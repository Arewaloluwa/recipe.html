ORANGE JUICE RECIPE

Orange juice is a citrus drink made plainly from raw oranges.

Recipe information

Preperation time: 20 mins

Serving: 4 people

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 10 oranges
  • ice cubes as preferred

Tools

Juice extractor

Instructions

  1. Peel all oranges
  2. Insert peeled oranges into the extractor
  3. extract juice into a jug
  4. served into separate cups
  5. add ice cubes as desired

Note

Best served chilled

BEST SERVED CHILLED
